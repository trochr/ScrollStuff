ScrollStuff

A bookmarklet to scroll up and down

Intro

In the great tradition of reinventing the wheel, here is ScrollStuff, a bookmarklet to scroll around a web page, in a continuous manner. Not unlike some middle mouse clicks, but with better precision.

The thing is unusable on a touch display, you need a cursor to use it.
